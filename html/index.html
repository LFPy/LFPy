LFPy documentation¶

LFPy is a Python package for simulation of extracellular electric field around neurons. It relies on the NEURON simulator and the Python interface to NEURON.

LFPy was developed at the Computational Neuroscience Group at the Norwegian University of Life Sciences, in collaboration with the Laboratory of Neuroinformatics, Nencki Institute of Experimental Biology, Warsaw, Poland. The effort was supported by International Neuroinformatics Coordinating Facility (INCF) and the e-science program of The Research Council of Norway.
